When React Flow is the right call

You're all-in on React, want the largest community and ecosystem in this space, and are happy assembling advanced behavior from its excellent examples. It's a great library — Grafloria's demo gallery deliberately covers the same ground so you can judge the overlap yourself.

When JointJS or GoJS is the right call

You need a vendor with commercial support contracts, SLAs, and decades-deep enterprise deployments. Both are mature, well-engineered commercial products.

When Grafloria is the right call

You want everything in the table in one MIT box — especially if your codebase is Angular or Vue, you need collaboration without standing up a sync stack, or you render diagrams on a server. And you can try every claim live before installing anything.
